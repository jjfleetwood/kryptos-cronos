## Trim candidates (ranked — pull only if tightening)

1. **Cobb interlude — para 2** (Act III, before *A Tuesday in July*). The catalog of Cobb's
   cruelties is the longest single block among the new interludes. If anything reads as
   "famous-players parade," this is the first place to shorten — could lose ~1–2 of the
   listed incidents and keep the stands-beating + the strike. *Est. -120 words.*

2. **News Comes Home / Hawk Comes Back burial overlap** (coda). Both narrate the procession
   up the hill in the rain to the graves of Inés + the first little Joaquín. *The Hawk Comes
   Back* needs the graveside only to land the hawk omen — its opening burial recap could be
   trimmed to a single clause so the procession is told once (in *News*). *Est. -40 words.*
   (De-dup, not a real cut — a clean flow win whenever you want it.)

3. **Meditation stacking: Widow → Fog → Night Sky → burial** (coda). Two lyric meditations
   between Rebecca's hard landing and the burial. I recommend KEEPING (best seam in the book
   is Night Sky→burial). But if it drags on a read-aloud, *The Fog* is the movable one →
   relocate to right after *The News Comes Home*.

4. **Act II interlude cluster: Matty · Merkle · The Benefit · Three Finger** sit close together
   (all "read in the agate type from afar"). You said keep all four. If one ever has to go for
   pace, *The Benefit* (Addie Joss) is the most self-contained — but it plants the withheld-from-
   his-widow payoff that *Eleanor* later pays off, so cutting it costs that echo. Cut last.

5. **Category-level reservoir:** the book carries many 3–4-paragraph nature/atmosphere
   meditations (*The Otters, The Whales, The Condor, The Chaparral, The Tide Pools, The Poppies,
   The Quail, The Hill, The River, The Creek*). Individually lovely; collectively the largest
   pool of cuttable length if a hard trim is ever needed. Plot/character chapters are NOT in
   this reservoir. (Needs a dedicated read to rank which 2–3 are the most skippable.)

## Structural edits made (Jacob-directed)

- **3→2 compression DONE.** Deleted standalone *The Redwoods*; folded its one unique beat
  (the felling image + "they never knew what they had") into *The Lime Kilns*. *The Powder
  Works* kept. Net ~-300 words, pace gain, and the redwood=Cirilo equation no longer pre-spent.
- **Oak → old-growth redwood (live-oak grove only).** Courtship grove (Stanford foothills,
  geologically valid — El Palo Alto is a coast redwood) now "a grove of old redwoods… the
  loggers had somehow missed" (echoes the finale tree); "oak dark"→"redwood dark"; "against
  an oak"→"against a redwood"; *The Margins* echo "oak grove"→"redwood grove". **Deliberately
  NOT converted:** rancho-fandango oaks (authentic oak-savanna shade trees in the protected
  beginning — you hang lanterns in a spreading oak, not a redwood column), tanning *oak bark*
  (factual tannin source), monarch grove (eucalyptus/pine, not redwood). Converting those
  would be "noticed." Say the word if you want them changed anyway.
- **New chapter "Under the Sun"** added before *The Redwood*: the burial day and the turning
  season rendered through the living coast (otter / gray whales / steelhead / quail / coyote /
  the vanished condor / the hawk) and the cycle of life — the earth taking him back with no
  question about his name. Returns the reader to the ground before the deep-time finale.
  Closing zoom-out now: human portraits → living coast (one year) → redwood (two millennia) →
  Epilogue (siempre).

## Flow edits made (non-cut, this pass)

- **Matty** (Act II): veiled the "the same year the fever came for Cirilo" / "the greaser…
  gone too soon" early death-reveal; recast as foreboding. Mathewson's real fate kept.
- **The Redwoods** (Act II): removed "lying in a cold room with his own felling near… he was
  a kind of redwood himself… felled late and spent fast and laid down young." This was both
  an early death-tell AND a preemption of the finale (*The Redwood*), whose whole purpose is
  to deliver the redwood-equation. Now reserved for the close; the social-arithmetic content
  of the chapter is untouched.
